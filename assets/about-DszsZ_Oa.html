About Jerech.

A modern, vibrant Zambian company offering a distinguished portfolio of mining supplies, civil works and engineered fabrication.

Our Story

Built on integrity, driven by ambition.

JERECH MINING AND CONSTRUCTION LIMITED is a wholly Zambian-owned company offering distinguished service and product delivery designed to compete in a fast moving industrial world.

We are well acquainted with modern business strategies and the corporate governance practices of leading industry giants — and we apply them daily to deliver outcomes our clients can trust.

Mission

To be the yardstick in our business ventures — distinguishing service and product delivery in the most economic and efficient way.

Vision

To redefine the exercise of effective and efficient delivery of goods and services that accurately match our customers' specific requirements.

Purpose

To create employment and career opportunities for Zambians, emphasising human resource development, capacity building and training.

Core Values

Radiance · Passion · Evolution

Radiance

Developing a commendable and noticeable corporate image in every interaction and project.

Passion

Dedicated service driven by ambitious customer satisfaction — at every touchpoint.

Evolution

Continuous innovation and development in service provision and product supply.
